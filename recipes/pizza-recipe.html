Classic Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Home

This recipe will guide you through making a cllassic pepperoni pizza from scratch. feel free to use store-bought dough and sauce if you are short on time.

Yields

  • 1 Large (14-16inch) Pizzaa

Prep Time

  • 25 minutes (plus 1.5-2hours for dough to rise)

Cook time

  • 12-15minutes

A great pizza starts with a great crust. This recipe creates a dough that's easy to work withand baakes up to be a crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside.

Part 1: The Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups(355ml) warm water (about 110*F)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon (1packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 2 tablesppons olive oil
  • 4 cups(480 g) all purpose or bread flour, plus more for dusting
  • 2 teaspoons fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Activate the yeast: In a large bowl, combine the warm water, yeast and sugar. Stir gently and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes foamy. This shows the yeast is active.
  2. Form the Dough: Add the olive oil, flour and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir with a spatula until a shaggy dough begiins to form.
  3. Knead the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a lightly flourded surgace. Knead for 8-10 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic. If using a stand mixer, use the dough hook and mix on low speed for 7-8 minutes.
  4. First Rise: Lightly oil a clean, large bowl. Place the dough in the bowl, turning it to coaat lightly with oil. Cover with a damp cloth or plastric wrap and let it rise in a warm place for 1-1.5hours, or until it has doubled in size.

Part 2: Simple & Flavoorful Pizza Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 (15-ounce) can crushed tomatoes or tomato purée
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine all the sauce ingredients.
  2. Stir until everything is well-mixed.
  3. Set aside. This sauce can be made ahead and stored in the fridge for up to a week.

Part 3: Assembling and Baaking Your Pizza

Now for the fun part - putting it all together!

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of prepared pizza dough
  • ½ cup of prepared pizza sauce
  • 2 cups (about 8 ounces) shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 4-5 ounces sliced pepperoni
  • Optional: A sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes, or fresh basil for garnish.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Place a pizza stone or an inverted baking sheet in your oven and preheat to the highest temperature possible, typically 475-500°F (245-260°C). Let the stone/sheet heat up for at least 30 minutes
  2. Shape the Dough: Punch down the risen dough to release the air. On a lightly floured surface, gently stretch the dough into a 14 to 16-inch circle. You can use your hands or a rolling pin.
  3. Top the Pizza: Place the shaped dough on a piece of parchment paper or a pizza peel lightly dusted with cornmeal. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Add Cheese and Pepperoni: Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top.
  5. Bake: Carefully slide the pizza (with the parchment paper, if using) onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Rest and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. This allows the cheese to set. Garnish with fresh basil or a sprinkle of Parmesan if desired.

Enjoy your delicious homemaade pepperoni pizza!
