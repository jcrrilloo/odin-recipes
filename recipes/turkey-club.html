Classic Turkey Club Sandwich Recipe

Prep

10 mins

Cook

30 mins

Total

40 mins

Servers

4

Ingredients

  • 16 slices center-cut applewood smoked bacon
  • 12 slices hearty white sandwich bread
  • 16(1/4-inch thick) slices vine-ripened tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 romain lettuce leaves
  • 16 slices deli turkey
  • 16 (4-inch) toothpicks

Directions

Bacon Instructions

  1. Pre-Heat oven to 400*F
  2. Get a baking pan
  3. Cover it with a baking sheet
  4. Arrange bacon slices onto baking sheet
  5. Put into pre-heated oven
  6. Bake bacon 15 - 20 minute, until browned and crisp
  7. Use tongs to transfer cooked bacon to a paper towel-lined plate to drain and reserve rendered fat.

White Sandwich Bread Instructions

  1. Brush bread slices very light ono both sides with reserved bacon fat
  2. Set on 2 clean baking sheets
  3. Working one baking sheet at a time
  4. Broill ono the upper middle rack in the oven to toast bread lightly
  5. Let toast for 1-2minutes
  6. Work both sides of the bun
  7. Remove from oven and work on second rack

Vegetables

  1. Sprinkle salt and black pepper on slices of tomatoes
  2. Add romain lettuce
  3. Additional Ideas: pickles, jalapenos

Placement

  1. Add your romain lettuce to one side of your sandwich bread
  2. Add your choice of toppings/vegetables
  3. Add bacon
  4. Add your choice of deli meat

Meat

  • Turkey Meat
  • Add 2 turkey slices on top of the bread slices of each prepared sandwich

    • Sauces and Seasoning

  • Your choice of additional home made sauce
  • Mayo
  • Thousand Island Sauce

    • Prepping

    1. Finish placing your sandwich together
    2. Plate your food
    3. Add your toothpicks on your sandwich

      4. Optional: Cut into mini slices
